🧘 Stress-Relief Tips
🎯
Focus on one debt at a timeAll extra payments toward #1 priority. One falling balance is far less stressful than spreading thin.
🚫
Freeze high-rate cardsAnything above 20% APR grows daily. No new charges while attacking them.
📅
Monthly review onlyUpdate once a month on a set date — not daily. Obsessive checking adds anxiety.
💪
Celebrate each payoffWhen a balance hits zero, celebrate cheaply, then roll that freed payment to the next target.
💳
Balance transfer opportunityA 0% intro APR card for your highest-rate balances pauses interest while you pay principal.
🌱
Low-rate debt is healthyMortgage and car loans under 5% are fine. Minimums are the right move.
